# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect all contributors and community members to:

- **Be respectful** — Disagree constructively
- **Be inclusive** — Welcome people of all backgrounds
- **Be professional** — Keep discussions focused on technology
- **Give credit** — Acknowledge ideas and solutions from others

## Acceptable Behavior

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experience levels
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what's best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive or demeaning language
- Unwelcome sexual attention
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Spamming or self-promotion outside designated channels

## Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:
1. Do **not** engage publicly
2. Email pritamdharme@gmail.com with details
3. Include context and any relevant messages

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Email communications
- Community discussions
- Any project-related interactions

## Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:
- Warning or temporary suspension
- Removal from the project

*By participating, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.*
